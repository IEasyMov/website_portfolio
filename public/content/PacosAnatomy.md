        <h2 style="font-size: 2rem; color: #007bff; margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">ðŸ‘¾ Gameplay</h2>
        <p style="margin-bottom: -1rem;">

        <p style="margin-bottom: -1rem;margin-top: 4.5rem;">
            <b style="color: #007bff;">The heart</b> - The heart is basically a pump that the player presses with their hand inside the hole. On the screen, a line appears with a heartbeat rhythm.
        </p>
        <p style="margin-bottom: -1rem;">You have to press the heart pump firmly each time the heartbeat reaches the line to make one beat.</p>
        <p style="margin-bottom: -1rem;">You need to complete as many beats in a row as shown on the example line. Each time you succeed, one symbol turns green. If you fail, everything resets.</p>
    </div>

        </p>
